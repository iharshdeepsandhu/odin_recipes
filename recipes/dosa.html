Dosa

Description

A dosa (also dosai or dosha or dose or thosai) is a thin pancake or crepe originating from South India, made from a fermented batter predominantly consisting of lentils and rice. ... Its main ingredients are rice and black gram, ground together in a fine, smooth batter with a dash of salt, then fermented.
